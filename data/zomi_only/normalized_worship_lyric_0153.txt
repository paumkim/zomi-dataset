=== Aw Zeisu Amen Hong Paita In
Cing Tom ===
Verse 1
Zeisu hong paikik nang, ciaptehna khempeuh leh
Pasian kamsangte genkholhte tung zo
Ih Topa Zeisu'n, ama vangliatna tawh
Mi siangthote manlang in hong khawmding
Chorus
Aw Zeisu, Amen hong paita in
A gimte khawlna hun hong tungta hen
Ngak lah ing, Amen hong paita in
It leh ngaih teng kimuhkiknading hun
Verse 2
Vanah ciaptehnate, hong tung kim khin zota
Ni mial, kha mial, aksite hong kia hi
Sangpen Topa maiah, lei leh van beimang zo
Topa hong paiding hun hong naita hi
Verse 3
Leitung hunsia khinzo, siatna hong khang semta
Kidona awging tampi kiza hi
Gitlohna hong khang in, huaiham angsung genna
Topa hong paiding hun hong naita hi
Verse 4
Hi gam lungdamna thu, leitung mun khempeuhah
Gen khit mateng bei na hong piangloding
kipum piak na nei in, deihsakna tawh sem ni
Hehpihna kong khak ma in zo meng ni
