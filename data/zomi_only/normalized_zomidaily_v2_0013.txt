News In Brief: Thuthak tomkim ~ The Tedim Post (28 Aug)\n
Evan Larboih in kum cinglo naupang 2 abuanna hang tawh thukhen zum in Kum 43 thong kiatding pan Kum 17ah khiam suk ahihman in Dingdi Numei Kipawlna in August 26, 2020 ni in deihloh lungphona Kawlpiah nei uh hi.\n
Rakhine State, Sittwe Khuapiah COVID-19 natna nei mipi a tamluatna hangin kumpi lam pan in zum nasepnaah 50% bek semtading hi, cih Rakhine State kumpi in August 25, 2020 in tangko uh hi.\n
2020 kiteelpiah Party dang tuamtuam pan leh mimal(individual) a demding Party members khempeuh party pan khawlsakna nei uh hi, ci in NLD party in August 26, 2020 ni in tangko uh hi.\n
August 19-21, 2020 sung Nay Pyi Taw akibawl a 4 veina Panlong khawmpi a Gambup thukimna pen August 26, 2020 ni in Pyidaungsu Hluttawah kipsakna nei uh hi. 2015-2020 kumpi hun sungin gambup thukimna 71 kinei thei hi.\n
2020 kiteelpi ading mee sazian pen a 2 veina pen September 20, 2020 ni in kisuang kikding hi, ci in Yangon Division a thunei ahi U Kyi Myint in gen hi.\n
Rakhine Stateah COVID-19 natna nasiat kik luatna hangin Rakhine State a om mipite khempeuh Stay at Homeding, ci in Cidamna lam thunei ten August 26, 2020 ni in tangko uh hi.\n
