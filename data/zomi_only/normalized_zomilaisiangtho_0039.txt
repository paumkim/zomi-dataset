Verse 1
A seem dingte tawm lua a, na sepding tampi om hi,
Topa buh lak hun zota hi.
Buhte minkhin kuai zota hi,
Tawntung khuamial gam sungah nisim in bei den,
Chorus
Aw, nisim nai simin bei den,
Ka itna bei thei tuanlo,
A mawkna in ka si luang ke'nteh,
Ka tatsate'n ahilh kei leh,
Vantung mi sawl mai ning.
Verse 2
Kuate ahiam, ka sawlding, kei adingin kua pai nuam,
Ka si manpha tawh ka tatsa,
Mawhna sungah nisim bei den,
Ka kiangah hong tung theiding kuamah omlo
Verse 3
Amaute ka it manin, ka gam nuampi nusia in,
Singkhuam tungah kipia zo ingh.
Gimna thuak a ka tatna thu,
Mi tampi'n theilo in khuamial sung om den.
Verse 4
Kei hiah om ingh Topa aw, nang ading hong sawl inla,
Ka nuntakna nang a hi khin,
Kha siangtho in hong makaih hen,
Ka sem dending gam khempeuh na neih mateng
