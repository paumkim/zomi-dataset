News In Brief: Thuthak tomkim ~ The Tedim Post (26 Sep)\n
Myanmar gamah Covid-19 natna hong tunzawh February kha pan August kha kikal North Shan Stateah Myanmar Army leh tualsuak minam thautawite kidona hangin mimal 50 val in sih lawh uh hi, ci in Shan State Gal vai tawh Dongtuak kipawlnaten gen uh hi.\n
South China Tuipi tung a apiang Nuri huihpi\/guahpi huzap in Myanmar gam Mon, Tanintharyi leh Pago Regionteah inn 130 kisia in mi 3 in sihlawh a mi 9000 val bang inn leh lo nusia in kihem uh hi.\n
Yangon Region Township 45ah Stay at Home thukhun hangin Myanmar gam a om thukizakna laihawm The Standard Times, 7Day News, Myanmar Times leh The Voice Daily cihte laihawm nasep tawmvei sung khawl bawl photding hi, cih thu kiza hi.\n
Rakhine Stateah Myanmar Army leh AAte galkidona khawl bawl nading leh internet line 4G hon sak kik nadingin Madalay Khuapiah lungphona anei All Burma Federation of Student Unions makai Ko Ba Chit pen thong kha 1 khiat dingin thukhen zumin thupia hi, cih thu kiza hi.\n
Gam khat peuh tawh thau vui\/thau tang suangin kidoding ka sawm kei uh hi, ci in September 22 ni akibawl UN Khawmpiah China Kumpi Xi Jinping thugenna video tungtawnin kiza hi. Hih khawmpiah US Kumpi Donald Trump in Covid-19 natna Leitung bup akizel sak China(sen) gam in vaipuak alak kulding hi, ci in gen ci hi.\n
Lebanon gam, Beirut khuapi pan tai 30 na a om Iran in apanpih Shia Muslim thautawi kipawlna Hezbollahte galvan koihna inn september 22 ni in puak kham a mi tampi in liamna ngah uh ci hi. A beisa August kha sungin zong Lebanon Khuapi Beirutah puahkhapna piang a mi 190 in sihlawh khin uh hi.\n
