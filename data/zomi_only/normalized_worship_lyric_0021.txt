=== Na Ziatlam Khut Tawh ===
Verse 1
A hoihna A vang-liatna gen zawh hilo
A muanhuai bel Topa
Hunte kikhel in a kisiat hang
Cik mah hun na kikhel ngei kei
Leitung maingatin, amah thudon kei zong
Hong itna kip lai zen
Nau bangin nong kepna lianlua e
Lungdam in Topa kong phat hi
Chorus
Na ziatlam khut tawh
Keimah nong makaih a, gun gal khatah
Haksatna guampi gal khat dongah
Na ciangkhut in kei
Mah lamnong lah manin
A bei theilo, athak itna tawh kei nong it a
A tul theilo, na hehpihna lian kong phat hi
Verse 2
Na sung ka, nuntak, a kipan tu ciang dong,
Awn suk in an nong pia,
Lungsim kha laulo in ka han na'ng
Nong pua hi, na liangko tungah
Ka lungsim tawng a, ka ngaihsut khempeuhte
Sitlo in nong pia a
Ka mailamding nong gelsak khempeuh
A sia omlo hoihna bek hi
