Guta a zong thei set
Tu LT hunah China te'n guta zon nading set (machine)
bawl uh, minute khat sungin guta mi sawm (10) zong thei.
Japan te'n kawm uh, minute 1 sungin guta nga (5) man uh.
Kawlgam te'n zong kawm uh, zongding a cih uh leh a zon
ma a kiguksak mawk!
\ Tawbo leh gil
Penglam hun lai nungak khat a tawbo deel lua, a veih a
thak ciang a nakvang tawh kituak lian. Pasal khat a gil puak
luain, a lup ciang a innvengte lupna tungah a gil kinga phei
liang.
Time machine
Mi khat in, “Time machine nei leng bel i Pu Zo pen Mongolia,
Tibet lam pan hong pai suk a, Sengam mun khat khawngah
tamsuaksak lelding kai ve! Kawlgam leh khamtung gam
peuhmahah lutsak loding hing. Tua hi leh ei zong Sente kisuak
dingin, ann limlim, meh limlim kineding! Middle East lam bel
thautui a hauh uh hang paisak nuam keng, i numeite bangzahta-
in melhoihta leh ki-en thei loding, i mit kamsiathuai lua” ci
mawk. “Zogam nuam, keng hong ngai lua ing,” ci khol kei leh
kilawm.
Gamkek di
Minam a ki-itsak mahmah pawlkhat in khantoh nading
thu kikum uh. Koici gam puah kipanding, ci in khatpa in
thu a pat leh, a lawmte khat in, “Gamkek di” ci gamgam se.

Stage 1-na
“Kum thak ciang za ngawlta ning” ciin a lawmte kiang
gen daidai. January 1 zan teh a lawmpa kiangah za ngen
dihdih. A lawmpa in, “Bang cia nang, za ngawl sawm hi lo na
maw?” a cih leh aman zong, “Ngawl luading tun stage 1-na
kipanta” ci. Alawmpa in, “Stage 1-na na cih bang ahia?” a cih
leh aman zong, “Za lei nawn loding!” civetse.
Tai a sawm galkap
Nidanglaite galkap tum nuam khat, a tha sang lua aih
cianga a lawmte khat in khel cih vem -
| Alawmpa : “Pii 6 nuai siah bek kilut thei ei, na lu lam
maw, na khe lam tanding?”
\ (ngaih sunsun ngeungau phot)
Galkap tum nuampa : “Bangbang ai zong, a na tateu zongin,
ka Iu lam mah tanzaw va'ng. Ka khe lam
hi leh, tai thei kei kha ning” peuh ci zanzan!
' Zukham kitawng
A : “Kei khua sungah a maih(mike) penpen, kuamah
kihtak nei lo!”
B : “Keiasoundbox penpen, kua mah lau tuan lo!”
Upalua
Mi khat zong upa lua in a deek paninPa (mushroom) po
citazenei! A thunget ciang nangawnin, “Aw Topa kei Na Pi”
cisawnsawn lail!
Vetse
Naupang no khat mi kongkhakah dakin, “KaPa Khup a
om hia?” ci in inntekte a dot leh inntek te'n, “KuaPa Khup a
hia?” ci in dongkik. Tua leh naupang in, “Thei lo maw
lawmlawm, Zam Lam Cin e, Zam Lam Cin, kaPa Khup ai ve”
civetse!

Pa : “Kata, bang hang kap na hia?”
Tapa : “Na zi (a nu) neih taakin hong vua hiven!”

Pa : “Naexam na uah na pass hia?”

Ta : “Khual Za Siam (apa min) ka fail kik zel”

Tu : “Puaw, bang laibu sim na hia?”
Pu : “History bu hi, bawi aw”
Tu : “Ahih lohpi pu aw, nu lepa (sex) bu ai ve leh”
Pu : “Kei adingin history ahi ve”
