=== Nuam Sing Topa
SOS Trio ===
Verse 1
Zingsang ka khanlawh theih nuam sing e
Ka khutte lamto in Topa ka phat
Ka khete lampai thei in, ka mitte'n khuamu thei
Ka aw ten Topa thupi'na tangko
Chorus
Nuam sing Topa nuntakna nong kop lai
Vangliatna tawh na kidim hi

Verse 2

Nang hang hi Topa, nang mah hang bek hi
