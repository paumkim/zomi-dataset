Who We Are
Zomi Laisiangtho is a community-driven website dedicated to serving the Zomi community by providing easy access to religious texts, hymns, and other community-focused resources. We&#x27;re guided by the values of faith, service, and community.
Our Journey
Started in 2023, our founder, Thang Tuang, recognized the need for an easily accessible online platform for Zomi religious texts and hymns. Ever since, we&#x27;ve been committed to refining and expanding our offerings, always keeping the needs of the Zomi community at heart.
Our Achievements
Over the years, we have curated and digitized numerous texts and hymns, serving as a valuable resource for our community. We&#x27;re proud of the growth of our user base and the positive feedback we&#x27;ve received from our community.
Our Future Plans
We aspire to take Zomi Laisiangtho a step further by developing a mobile application, making these resources more accessible than ever. However, such an undertaking is costly, and we would appreciate your support. If you would like to contribute to this endeavor, please contact us. Your support can make a significant difference in our community.
