KOH MASAK THU
ZABATSS Vol. 3naah “Koh Masak Thu” a gelh dingin hong
kiseh manin ka nuam mahmah hi. Kei tumtak in za ngah kisa mahmah
in, angtang mahmah ing.
Hi ZABATSS Vol.3 na sunga kigelh thusun tuamtuam teng a
tomin lom et suk pak ning ci a ka pat leh, hoihsa theilua ka hih manin
khia thei nawn lo in, a tawp dong ka sim suak sitset hi. Ka theihsa pawl
khat hong phaw kik sak in, ka theih ngei nai ngiatloh thu thak leh thugil
tampi tak ka theih lawh a, laigelh ih mipilte pil takpi, thei takpi mawk
uh ei ci in ka omna mun pan laigelh teng pahtawina lianpi ka pia hi.
Laigelhte in thu theih huai tampi hong gelh khiat tek ban vuah
amau muhdan leh amau deihsak dan ciat tawh thu hong khup diam
diam uh a, ngaihsutnate zong thuk thei sa mahmah in, Zogam leh Zomite
mailamding nuntak gim hong namsak in, lametna lianpi hong pia ahih
manin; Pasian bawlsa Zomite a mangthang nawn hetloding ih hih bek
thamloh a khangto taktak leh a khangto pha mahmah laiding ih hihna
hong musak hi. Tua ahih manin hi ZABATSS pen Zomite nuntak kalsuan
danding lamlak ‘AksiPi khat' hi ci in ka mu hi.
Laisiam mipilte in, mi khat peuh in Doctrate (Ph.D. etc.) a ngah
khit ciangin lai a sim beh nawn kei leh a thutheihna pen a Doctrate ngah zawh
kum nga khit teh mi ban nawnlo hi ci uhhi. Leitungah pilna pen ni khan
keei mai cih hong musak hi. Hih sunga laigelh sianu siapate pen tunai
diak a Doctrate ngahte, tu laitak a asin san a zo dektakte leh, M.Th.
hiam a sang mahmah khat ciat peuhmah a zo tingteng a lai a simsim
laite vive uh ahih manin hong thusunte uh tanglai thu pek pan tuni
tunai penpen a kimu khia pilnate tawh hong hial diamdiam uh a,
pilna tomkim leh bukim tak in hong suah uh ahih man manpha lua sa
in lamdang bang ka sa liang hi.

Tua ahih manin hih laibu pen Lai Siangtho sin ngei peuhmah
in bu khat ciat lei hamtang a, a tawm pen vial khat ciat beek ih sim
pelmawhding kong hanthawn nuam mahmah hi. Lai Siangtho a sin
laitakte in zong lei in na tel mateng uh sim le uh cin, Mangpau a ih
zak, Manglai a ih sim a ih tel zawh kholloh thuthuk tampite hih laibu
sungah ei pianpih pau in tom leh kicing tak in hong kigelh ahih manin
lei in sim lecin Lai Siangtho sanga na sin thu tampi telbeh na in nei
ding in kong lamen hi. Pello in lei ciat ni.
Lai Siangtho sang a kah ngeilote leh a kah man nailote in
zong lei in sim leng Lai Siangtho leh Pasian Thugil luanzia hih cidan hi
mawk maw ci in, thu thuk muhna leh lungsim kihonna in nei dingin
kong lamen a, Lai Siangtho sang kah lo thu um mite a dingin zong
thupi kan lai dingin ka um hi. Mikim a dinga hoih laibu hi.
