=== Itna Sisan

Esther (Sian Ki Cing) ===
Verse 1
Khuamial sung a, mangthang mite
Lamet ding neilo, lam khial mite
Itna in ei hong zong khia hi
Itna hang in sisan hong luang khia hi

Chorus
Na itna in hong phong to hi, hong lam to hi
Mawhna khuamial sung si ding kim lai
Na nunna nong pia maw kei lel
Lungdam na lathak sa in
Na min kong pahtawi hi

Verse 2
A mangthang te, honkhia ding in
Van hauhna, nopna te nusia in
Itna tawh kei, hong zong kik hi
A zawngkhal te khat bek zong nusia lo

Verse 3
It huai lo kei, lel nong it man
Singkhuam tung ah sihna nong thuak maw
Minthanna kumpi, lukhu nusia in
Ling lukhu leh kei nong tel zaw hi
