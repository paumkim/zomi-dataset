Lawm Khup Khan Thang ong kuppih dan in hoih isak tek gen pong mawk ni. Kiseel leh kiko kha het kei ni ci-in ong thuum khol ing. Tuma kum 20 val lai in ei Zomi MUKO Magazine abawlden khat in hun kikheel leh ei Zogam makai ding kua hoih nasa hiam cih ong interview ngei hi. Ken ah ami in ong gen kei zaw ning in athu in ong gen ning kaci hi.<\/p>\n
1. A kum pen 30 nuai hilo ding. Kum 60 val zong hikei zaw leh<\/li>\n
2. Laitan pilna pen atawm pen in Degree kician tak in angah khat hi in, Zokam, Kawlkam ahihtheih laileh Mang kam zong siam leh<\/li>\n
3. Kumpi nasep, mitawh kiholhna atawmpen in kum sawm bang asem khinsa, mitetawh kipawl thei te<\/li>\n
4. Agamtat luheekna ah paubanna a omlo mi.<\/li>\n
5. Mi deidanna, etsatna a neilo mi.<\/li>\n
6. Gam it tatak a ahuaiham hetlo mi.<\/li>\n
7. A biak Pasian a zahtaak mahmah mi cibang hileh nang tua tawh nasaikak veleh kaci ngei hi.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

Zomi a it vinven pa hi.<\/p>\n

Eimi Politics asem ngei katheihkhak sung pan kazahtaak mahmahte ahi uh hi.<\/p>\n
