=== Tuunnu Aw Lungdam Ing

Suih Lun ===
Verse 1
Tuunnu aw nong itna
Tehpih theih ding om kei
Simlei ah kei nong it bang
Hong it om kei
Gual tawh tan bang kim na’ng
Pilna sang nong kah sak a
Nong itna, nong deihsakna te lian lua e

Chorus
Tunnu aw lungdam ing
Nang bang nu kong neih khak man
Simlei ah kei nong it bang
Hong it om kei
Laisiangtho hong sinpih in
Sian sinthu tawh nong pattah
