“A day without laughter is a day wasted” - Charles Chaplin

Pasian bawl na khempeuhte lakah lungdamna hanga a
nuikhia thei mihingte bek hi (“laughing hyena” leh “laughing
dove” kicite lungdamna hang a nui hi lo). Tua ahih manin i
omna peuhpeuha mi lungdam nading a gen maw i hih, mi heh
nading leh lungkimloh nading a genden? cih ngaihsuthuai
mahmah hi. Mi khat in, “Kei bel mi in ka pilna thu a genloh uh
hangin, ka kiangah om nuam a sakna thu gen uh hi” ci hi. Mi
te’n ‘pil’ a cihloh uh hangin a gen lo uh hi bek hi. Mi haite
kiangah om nuam a sa tam lo ding hi!

Thugensiamte’ thugensiamna hang ka ngaihsut ciangin -
a thugen thulu, a kamsiam, a aw luanzia, leh a thungaikhia
mipite in tampi sai mah leh, tuate sanga lianzaw a hang khat

: om a, tua pen a thugenna sung uah etsak nading leh genteh
nadinga mite nuih theih nading kammal a zatsiamna hang hi,
' cih kei muhna ahi hi.

Zomi sungah thugen siam ka sak - Evan. Langh Do Khup,
~ Rev. Pau Khan Khai, Rev. Job Thawngno, Rev. Dr. Gin Khan
Khual, Evan. Zir Lian Sangate pen midang in zong siamsa uh
hi ding hi, gam tuamtuamah thugen dingin sam kawikawi uh
a, a hun uh kicing zo mahmabh lo hi. Tuate in a thugenna sung
uah phumsim, selsim, mi nautangte zatsiam mengmengloh

ciamnuih kammal leh gentehnate zangsiam uh hi.

Thugelh lamah cileng, gensawm kibang tektek napi, mi
lungsim a bun mahmah, mi te’n sim nuam a sak theih nading-
a a thugelh sung ua ciamnuih kammal leh gentehna a phumsim
siamte thugelh pen sim nuam mahmah hi. Rev. Job. Thawngno,
Rev. Go Khen Thang, Vanneihtluanga, Darrokima, Mafaa-te
pawl in hih banga gelh siam uh a, tua manin a laibu bawlte uh

bilbel a, gelhkhialh, genkhialh leh a man kei deuh leh zong
ciamnuih hi pah lel hi.

Hih laibu sunga ciamnuih tamzaw pen gensa mah bangin
facebook a Ciamnuih group pana kila hi a, hih group a contri-
bution a pia hoih mahmah - (facebook min bangin) Hau Za
Cin Suante, David Ngalliam, Mungpi Khupson, Tg Samuel,
Phuitong Zomi, Ruthie SB, Zogam Tangval, Ngaihte Asingpi, Pu
Samte, Jasuan Lianthang, En Dim-te tungah lungdam ka ko
hi. Hih lawm teng pen Ciamnuih University bang om hi leh
ciamnuih subject khat peuhpeuhah doctorate degree a la thei
ding vive ahi uh hi. Amaute banah a min ka gelhloh USA,
Australia, Malaysia, Singapore, India, Germany, Myanmar,
leh gam tuamtuama ciamnuih memberte khempeuh tungah
zong lungdam ka ko hi. Tua banah laibu pawlkhat pana ka
kaikhop zong om a, kei’ phuaktawm pen tam kihel het lo hi.
Ciamnuih gen/gelh siam hi dingin ciamnuih lamah ‘talent’
neih hoih mahmah kisam leh kilawm!

i ‘Ciamnuih’ laibu a pian theih nadinga tha le zung,
ngaihsutna leh hun tampi zang a, hong hanthawn a tha hong
piate khempeuh tungah ka lungdam a, facebook ‘Ciamnuih’
group administrator Mungpi Khupson leh Dr. Hau Za Cin in
‘lungdampihna’ leh ‘deihsakna’ hong gelh uh ahih manin
amaute gel tungah lungdamna lian mahmah hi.

Ciamnuih om kei leh a si thei phial dinga kingaihsun,
ciamnuih siam minthang khat in a nuihloh ni pen ‘kimanna
om lo ni hi’na ci ziau a, makaih siamna tawh kisai a mite theih
mahmah J. Oswald Sanders in, “Makai ciamnuih siam lote
lawhcing thei taktak lo hi” na ci hi.

Tua ahih manin hih laibu a sim khempeuh a metpih ding
leh a nuihpih zo ngiat dingin kong deihsak hi.
